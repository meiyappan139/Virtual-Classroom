Interview Experience

          Written Test:


          * This Round has 2 sections. Each section has a sectional cut-off. 
          * They evaluate the 2nd section of the paper only if the candidate had qualified the 1st section with the required cut-off marks.
             
            

           Section 1: 

           The first round consists of 10 questions.

           Physics(Electro magnetic, Projectile, Energy, Power)

           Maths(Area, Volume, Velocity, Aptitude)

           C programming(Pointers)

           Electronic devices, Embedded systems,12th  physics like velocity, acceleration related questions. 
           They provide some basic formulas like v=u+at. Mass and weight related problems. Frequency related problems
           

           Section 2: 

           In this round, you are provided with 3  Bonus questions which carry of additional marks to solve logical puzzles, 
           math tricks, probability, riddles, circuit designing(kcl, kvl) with a time relaxation of  30 minutes to 60 minutes.
           where group discussion and technical interview will happen in parallel.
