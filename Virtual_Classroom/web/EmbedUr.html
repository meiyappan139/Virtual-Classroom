        Test Description

          Embed Ur Systems Interview process has the following round:
         
          • Written test
         
          • Group discussion/ Just a minute / Extempore
         
          • Technical Interview
         
          • Hr Interview
         
          The first round consists of 35 simple question from Theoretical Networking, Microprocessor, C Programming, Aptitude and verbal.

          It is a written test where you have to mark your answers on OMR sheets.

          The second round is extempore round (you can choose your own topic) and the third round is FTF technical interview.

Interview Tips

          If you can code, you have a place at Embed Sytems but to show them that you can code you have to clear the first round. 

          This is possible only if you get some practice. 

          We strongly recommend you to practice well and advise you to not miss the opportunity to work with a company solely due to the lack of practice.
