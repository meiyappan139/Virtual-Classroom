Interview Process


          Recruitment Process :

          JUSPAY conducts 3-5 rounds to select freshers as Software Engineer in their organisation.

          1.Aptitude Round
          2.Online Coding Round
          3.Technical Round 1
          4.Technical Round 2
          5.HR Round

          Academic Criteria :

          70 percent or above in B.Tech, Class X and XII.

          No backlogs at the time of interview


          Written Round :

          The Aptitude round consists of three major sections namely General Aptitude Test, Verbal Ability Test and Technical Test. General Aptitude Test, 
          has mathematical reasoning part and logical reasoning part.


          Online Coding Round :

          The students who clear the aptitude round are called for coding round. To clear this round you should be strong with data structures and algorithms. 

          You should be prepared Stack, queue, Tree, and Graph along with code optimization.

          Technical Rounds :

          The students who clear the coding round are called for Technical Interview.To clear this round you should be clear with your basics. 

          You should be prepared with Data structures and Algorithms, DBMS, and OOPs concepts. Students will be expected to write codes in the interview. 

          You could be expected to optimize your previous round codes. You can expect questions from resume. You may be asked puzzles in this round. 

          To be prepared for puzzles you can practice from our Puzzles section.


         HR Round :

          Sometimes they only ask HR questions like :
          1. Tell me about Yourself
          2. Why JUSPAY ?
          3. How you see yourself after five years from now ?
          4. What are your strengths and weaknesses ?
          5. Questions form resume
