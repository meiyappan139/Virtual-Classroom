Interview Experience


          Round-1

            The first round comprised of 40 MCQ questions including aptitude, logical, C output questions and technical portions and 5 programming questions(Test Duration: 90 mins) :-
            Technical aptitude part covered questions from computer networks, operating system, data structures,database management system and software engineering.
            You can solve the programming questions in c,c++,java ,python.          

           Round-2 

            Round 2 involves technical interview.
            Usually Trimble conducts at least three technical interviews.
            Prepare the concepts of your areas of interest.
            Questions are asked in data structures and database management system(normalization and joins)
            Prepare puzzles from geeks for geeks.
            Puzzles are asked to check your logical thinking


           Round-3: 

            Personal interview is all about your family,goals and interests.
            Questions raised are
            Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
            What is your strength and weakness?
            
            
           Tips: Cracking Trimble interview is simple if you are well prepared in database(sql) and data structures.
